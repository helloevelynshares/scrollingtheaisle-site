How we decide what's worth it

Compare the unit price

Package sizes lie. We normalize to per-ounce, per-pound, or per-item so a “sale” is actually cheaper.

Check against Costco

When there's a warehouse match, we line up grocery promos against regional Costco pricing — not random cross-store guesses.

Watch what people actually buy every week

We track recurring staples — berries, chips, yogurt, eggs — and flag when prices dip vs. their own history.
